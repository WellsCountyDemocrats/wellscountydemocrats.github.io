Know Your Deadlines

Indiana Voting Deadlines

These are general Indiana deadlines, always verify specific dates at indianavoters.in.gov for each election.

Action Deadline
Voter Registration (in person) 29 days before Election Day
Voter Registration (online) 29 days before Election Day
Absentee Application Deadline 12 days before Election Day (by mail)
Early / Absentee In-Person Voting Begins 28 days before Election Day
Election Day Polls Open 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM (local time)
⚠️ Reminder: Indiana requires a valid photo ID to vote. Acceptable IDs include Indiana driver's license, state ID card, U.S. military ID, or U.S. passport. Your name must appear on the ID.
