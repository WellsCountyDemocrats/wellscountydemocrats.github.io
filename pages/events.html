Let's Talk Tuesday

Every Tuesday, Wells County Democrats gather to do what democracy runs on — conversation. Let's Talk Tuesday is an informal, open meetup where neighbors come together to discuss current events, share ideas, organize around issues that matter, and simply connect with like-minded people in our community. No agenda, no pressure — just good people having real conversations about the things that affect our lives here in Wells County. Whether you're a lifelong Democrat or just curious about getting involved, you're welcome at the table.

Democracy works better when we talk to each other.
