Neighborhood Leaders

Precinct Chairs

Precinct chairs are the most important volunteers in our party, connecting neighbors with the Democratic Party and turning out the vote in every corner of Wells County.

What is a Precinct Chair?

Each of Wells County's 22 precincts can have a Democratic precinct chair, a neighborhood leader who serves as the local face of the party, helps register voters, and drives turnout on election day. We are actively recruiting chairs for all precincts.

What We Believe

Our Democratic Platform

Democrats believe we're greater together than we are on our own. Here's what we're fighting for in Wells County.

💼

Jobs & Economy

We support good-paying jobs, fair wages, and an economy that works for every family, not just those at the top. Equal pay for equal work is non-negotiable.

🏥

Health Care

Every person deserves access to affordable, quality health care. We support expanding coverage and protecting those with pre-existing conditions.

🎓

Education

Strong public schools and access to higher education are cornerstones of a thriving community. We support our teachers and students at every level.

🌿

Clean Energy

We believe in protecting our environment for future generations and investing in clean energy solutions that create jobs right here in Indiana.

⚖️

Equal Rights

Every person deserves to be treated with dignity and respect regardless of who they are, where they're from, or who they love.

🗳️

Democracy

Free and fair elections are the foundation of our republic. We support every eligible citizen's right to vote and have their voice heard.
