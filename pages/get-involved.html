Get Involved

Democracy is not a spectator sport. Here's how you can make a real difference in Wells County.

🙋
Make a Difference

Volunteer

The Wells County Democratic Party runs on volunteers like you. Whether you have 2 hours or 20, there's a meaningful way to contribute to our community and our cause.

Ways to Volunteer

  • Help at community events and rallies
  • Assist with voter registration drives
  • Distribute literature and yard signs
  • Support candidate campaigns
  • Help with data entry and office work
  • Join our phone and text banking efforts

No experience necessary. We will train you. What matters is your enthusiasm and commitment to Wells County.

Ready to volunteer? Fill out the form below and we'll be in touch to find the right fit for you.

Reach Voters

Phone & Text Banking

One of the most effective ways to reach voters is direct conversation. Our phone and text banking volunteers contact Wells County residents to share information about candidates, issues, and how to vote.

What to Expect

  • Short training provided. No cold-calling experience needed
  • Work from home on your own schedule
  • Use our provided scripts and talking points
  • Make a real, measurable difference in election outcomes

Sign up for our next phone/text bank session. We'll reach out with session details.

Run for Office

Neighborhood Leadership

Precinct Chairs

Precinct chairs are the backbone of the Democratic Party. As a precinct chair, you serve as the local Democratic leader in your neighborhood, connecting neighbors with the party and turning out the vote on election day.

What Precinct Chairs Do

  • Serve as the party's representative in your precinct
  • Help register voters in your neighborhood
  • Distribute voter information and candidate materials
  • Encourage Democrats in your precinct to vote
  • Attend county party meetings and caucuses
  • Be the face of the Democratic Party for your neighbors

Who Can Be a Precinct Chair?

Any registered Democrat living in Wells County can become a precinct chair. It's one of the most impactful things you can do for your party and your community.

Interested in becoming a precinct chair? Contact us and we'll walk you through everything.
